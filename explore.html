Barbican Estate

The Barbican Estate, or Barbican, is a residential complex in City of London. This iconic brutalist architecture is a must-see local landmark.

Sir John Soane's Museum

Sir John Soane's Museum is a house museum near Holborn Station. This quirky museum is home to thousands of paintings, sculptures, drawings, and antiquities.

Leather Lane

Leather Lane is a street west of Hatton Garden in Holborn. There are many places to grab a nice coffee and a wide range of street food.
